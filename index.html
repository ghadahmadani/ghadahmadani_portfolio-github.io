The Escape

30MFF Website

"The Escape" is a short film that follows the gripping journey of a university student faced with an unexpected situation – a fire alarm disrupting her seemingly ordinary day on campus. This project delves into the heart-pounding chaos and tension that happens as she endeavors to make her way to safety, navigating through a maze of corridors, stairwells, and exits. The overall concept of "The Escape" revolves around the theme of resilience, quick thinking, and the determination to overcome adversity in a high-stress situation. It highlights the innate human instinct to survive and adapt, even in the most unexpected and challenging circumstances. The central character, the uni girl, becomes a symbol of the everyday hero, and her story serves as a metaphor for the trials and tribulations faced by students as they strive to complete their education while tackling unexpected obstacles. The project aims to create an immersive and suspenseful experience for the audience. Viewers are transported into the chaotic world of the university campus during a fire alarm. Overall, "The Escape" is a thrilling and thought-provoking project that combines storytelling, suspense, and interactivity to create an immersive experience that resonates with audiences. It explores themes of resilience and survival while celebrating the unbeatable spirit of individuals facing unexpected challenges in their pursuit of education and personal growth.

Beyond the Gray

Interactive Comic

Davo, a middle-aged man trapped in the monotony of his office life, discovers an old map within the office files. Fueled by a desire for adventure beyond the grind of spreadsheets, he ventures into a mysterious forest. As Davo navigates the greenery, he encounters ancient trees and unforeseen challenges that become opportunities for growth. In the heart of the forest, he discovers an ancient tree that imparts a profound revelation, providing him with a new life purpose. Returning to the office, Davo transforms the once-dull workspace, inspiring his co-workers on a journey of self-discovery. The story unfolds as a captivating narrative of escape from routine, self-discovery, and the transformative power of nature.

Behind the Camera

Video Project

In the captivating interactive film Behind the Camera, the once ordinary daily life of a student takes a mysterious turn when he becomes convinced that someone is following him. As he goes about his usual routine, he becomes increasingly aware of bizarre happenings, such as objects mysteriously appearing, instances of gaslighting that leave him questioning his own sanity, and an uncanny sensation of being constantly observed. Will he be able to maintain his sanity amidst unsettling incidents, or will he be driven to delve deeper into the enigmatic forces that are fueling his mounting paranoia?
